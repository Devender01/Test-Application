WELCOME TO UTTARAKHAND⛰️

Uttarakhand, often called the "Land of Gods" or Devbhumi, is a picturesque state in northern India, nestled in the lap of the Himalayas. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, it boasts snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, and sacred rivers like the Ganga and Yamuna. The state is famous for its spiritual significance, with renowned pilgrimage sites such as Haridwar, Rishikesh, Kedarnath, and Badrinath attracting devotees from across the world. Apart from spirituality, Uttarakhand is a haven for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts, offering trekking, river rafting, and wildlife experiences in national parks like Jim Corbett and Valley of Flowers. Its unique blend of cultural heritage, serene environment, and natural beauty makes Uttarakhand a truly enchanting destination.



Mussoorie, known as the "Queen of Hills," is a charming hill station in Uttarakhand, India. Nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas at an altitude of about 2,000 meters, it offers breathtaking views of the Doon Valley and snow-capped mountains. Famous for its pleasant climate, scenic landscapes, and colonial architecture, Mussoorie is a favorite destination for tourists seeking peace, nature, and adventure.

The Places to visit are:

  1. Kempty Falls
  2. Camel’s Back Road
  3. Gun Hill
  4. Lal Tibba

Kempty Falls: A stunning waterfall where visitors can enjoy a refreshing dip.

Camel’s Back Road: A scenic walking trail with panoramic views of the mountains.

Gun Hill: The second highest point in Mussoorie offering a spectacular viewpoint.

Lal Tibba: The highest point in Mussoorie with breathtaking sunrise and sunset views.



Five Places in Dehradun to visit:

  1. Robber’s Cave (Guchhupani)
  2. Sahastradhara
  3. Mindrolling Monastery
  4. Tapkeshwar Temple
  5. Forest Research Institute (FRI)

Robber’s Cave (Guchhupani): A natural cave formation with a river flowing inside.

Sahastradhara: Famous for its waterfalls and sulphur springs.

Mindrolling Monastery: A large Buddhist monastery known for peace and beauty.

Tapkeshwar Temple: A scenic cave temple beside a river.

Forest Research Institute (FRI): Stunning colonial architecture and nature.
